Unofficial Parkrose Heights Homepage

Neighborhood Association

The Parkrose Heights Association of Neighbors (PHAN) appears to be defunct. You can still see its official page on the East Portland Community Office website. Its Facebook page is here. (Note: parkroseheights.org used to be the website for PHAN, but they let it lapse. This site is not affiliated with PHAN or any other organization).
